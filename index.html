Managing the portfolio

Process beats prediction. These keep an equal-weight book disciplined.
01

Hold the weights

Each position is sized at 10% on purpose. Trim winners back toward 10% and redistribute to laggards.

02

Rebalance monthly

The model re-ranks on a 1-month clock. Stick to the cadence; act off-schedule only on a broken thesis.

03

Keep cash in reserve

A small reserve lets you add into selloffs instead of just watching.

04

Let it compound

The edge is in repeating the process, not any single trade. Give it time.
